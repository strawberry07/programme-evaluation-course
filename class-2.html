Class 2: Stakeholder Analysis and Engagement

Date: 12/1

Readings: Royse et al. Ch. 4 & 7

Intended Learning Outcomes

By the end of this lecture, you are expected to be able to:

  • Elaborate the essence of accountability in programme evaluation
  • Recognize importance of involving stakeholders and key strategies (i.e., stakeholder analysis)
  • Articulate the key ethics principles in programme evaluation
  • Recognize the importance of cultural competence in programme evaluation

Topics Covered

  • Stakeholder analysis
    • Identification
    • Grouping and Prioritization
    • Understanding
  • Stakeholder engagement plan
  • Ethics
  • Accountability
  • Cultural competence
