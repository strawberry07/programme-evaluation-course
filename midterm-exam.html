Mid-Term Examination

Students must sit for a mid-term examination. The examination questions may be set in forms of True/False statements, multiple choice questions, fill-in-blank questions, and short-answered questions.

Students are encouraged to read not only the lecture notes but also recommended materials to deepen understandings of the course content.

Examination Format

  • True/False statements
  • Multiple choice questions
  • Fill-in-blank questions
  • Short-answered questions

Preparation Tips

  • Review all lecture notes thoroughly
  • Read recommended materials from the course schedule
  • Review chapters from Royse et al. and Grinnell et al. textbooks
  • Focus on understanding key concepts and evaluation methods
  • Practice with course examples and case studies
