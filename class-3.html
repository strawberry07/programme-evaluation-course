Class 3: Needs Assessment

Date: 19/1

Readings: Royse et al. Ch. 3 Or Grinnell et al. Ch. 11 (Ch. 10 in 7th edition)

Intended Learning Outcomes

By the end of this lecture, you are expected to be able to:

  • Elaborate the critical importance of needs assessment
  • Identify four types of needs based on Bradshaw's Typology
  • Practice problem analysis and service user analysis

Topics Covered

  • Definition of Needs
  • Typology of Needs:
    • Normative needs
    • Comparative needs
    • Expressed needs
    • Felt needs
  • Problem Analysis
  • Understand Service Users
