Class 4: Evidence-based Practice (EBP)

Date: 26/1

Readings: Royse et al. Ch. 1 Section: Evidence-based Practice (EBP)

Intended Learning Outcomes

By the end of this lecture, you are expected to be able to:

  • Define and elaborate the importance of evidence-based practice (EBP) in social work services
  • Identify theory of change in a social service programme and its relationship with logic model

Topics Covered

  • Evidence-based practice (EBP)
  • Hierarchy of Evidence
    • Evidence Locating
  • Theory of Change
  • Assumption of a programme
